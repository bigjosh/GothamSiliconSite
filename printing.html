Ultra High Resolution Printing Services

Gotham Silicon is offering a limited run of the highest resolution commercially available printing service. Immortalize your data, text, or images at an unprecedented resolution. We utilize advanced lithography techniques to create stunning, high-density prints on a quartz substrate.

The Process

Our process involves embossing your design into a thin layer of chromium metal that sits atop a transparent quartz substrate. The chromium blocks light, creating a permanent, high-contrast black and white image. We achieve a remarkable resolution of 33,866 DPI, which corresponds to a pixel size of just 750nm by 750nm. These photomasks are the same kind used in industrial lithography processes to transfer patterns onto photosensitive materials.

Unparalleled Density

  • 4.75" x 4.75" design space on a 5" x 5" substrate
  • Total pixel footprint of 25,878,012,676 pixels (over 25,000 megapixels)
  • 750nm x 750nm raster size (33,866 DPI)
  • Capacity for over a billion characters with a compact font like Zepto (8px)

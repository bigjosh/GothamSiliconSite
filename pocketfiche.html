We are launching a strange device, it's not silicon but it is nano

Pocket Fiche is an information technology for communicating to future generations. I setup the simple nanofabrication process required to print into gold at the micron scale and my friends at CW&T did the macroscopic design. The result is the densest and longest lasting human readable information storage media. We considered what Pocket Fiche should preserve in time/communicate to the future and landed on letting you decide. We are making available our device along with rights to control a fraction of the printed content it will contain so that you can make collective art million dollar homepage style. However unlike the million dollar home page and other forms of digital/cloud based information technology Pocket Fiche does not rely on complicated, constantly evolving, and vulnerable (on the millennia timescale) infrastructure to persist across time, and unlike the pages of a book, the canvas of a painting, or the bits in a hard drive, the materials that make a Pocket Fiche have be specifically chosen to last thousands of years so that the message of every contribution will be preserved for future humans to observe. The device only assumes its viewer has working eyes and a source of light.

I encourage you to think hard about what you would communicate to people or a specific person in the future. Anytime from a year from now to 2871. Once your ready go on and say it.
